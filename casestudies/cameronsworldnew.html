Welcome to Candies(ice)'s World!!

Cameron's World is a poignant tribute to the early days of the internet, captured through a web-collage of text and images extracted from archived GeoCities pages (1994–2009). This project, meticulously curated by Cameron Askin and supported by Anthony Hughes and Robin Hughes, revives a digital era often overshadowed by the polished, branded content of today.

GeoCities, once a vibrant platform allowing users to craft their web pages, hosted over 38 million sites before its closure in 2009. Cameron's World weaves fragments from these thousands of pages, offering a nostalgic glimpse into a time when personal web spaces were works in progress—an era marked by unrefined, individual expression.

I am deeply moved by Cameron's World and its evocative web-collage approach. It brings to light the charm of an internet long gone, filled with the raw and unfiltered creativity of its early users. It is a powerful reminder of a time when the internet was less commercialised and more about genuine, albeit sometimes chaotic, self-expression. Cameron's World preserves this fragmented digital history and serves as a heartfelt reminder of a time when the internet was a canvas for personal creativity and exploration. Inspired by this, as a lover of collages, I created my own ‘world’ on the site.
